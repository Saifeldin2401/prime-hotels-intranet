## 1. Introduction
The Knowledge Base is the centralized hub for all PRIME Hotels operational standards, policies, and procedures. It ensures that every team member, from frontline staff to corporate executives, has access to the same "Single Source of Truth."

**Key Benefits:**
- **Standardization:** Ensure consistent service across all properties.
- **Accessibility:** Instant access to SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) on any device.
- **Compliance:** Track who has read and acknowledged critical updates.

## 2. Browsing & Searching Content

### Home Dashboard
Access the Knowledge Base from the sidebar. The home page features:
- **Featured Articles:** High-priority or recently promoted content.
- **Required Reading:** Documents assigned specifically to you that require acknowledgment.
- **Browse by Type:** Quickly jump to SOPs, Policies, Guides, or Checklists.

### Searching
Use the global search bar at the top of the Knowledge Base. You can search by:
- Article titles
- Keywords within the content
- Department names

**Pro Tip:** Use the **Filter** section in the Search page to narrow results by Department or Document Type.

## 3. Creating & Editing Content
*Note: Creation permissions depend on your assigned role (e.g., Department Heads, HR, or Admins).*

### üß± Basic Information
To create a new article, click the **"Create Article"** button in the Knowledge Base header.
1.  **Title:** Be clear and descriptive (e.g., *"Front Desk Check-in Procedure"*).
2.  **Type:** Select the appropriate category (SOP, Policy, Checklist, etc.).
3.  **Description:** A 1-sentence summary of what the article covers.
4.  **TL;DR Summary:** Professional summary for staff who need a quick refresh without reading the full details.

### ‚úçÔ∏è Content Editor

- Format text (Bold, Italics, Headings).
- Insert bullet points and numbered lists.
- Add links to other internal or external resources.

### üìé Attachments & External Links
- **External URL:** Link to external training videos or corporate resources.
- **Upload File:** You can upload **PDF** documents directly. These will be securely stored and viewable within the platform.

## 4. AI Content Assistant
PRIME Connect includes an **AI Assistant** (powered by Gemini) to help maintain professional standards.

- **Generate Outline:** Provide a title, and the AI will create a structured outline for your SOP.
- **Expand Content:** Turn your rough notes into full, professional paragraphs.
- **Improve Writing:** Automatically polish grammar, tone, and clarity.
- **Bilingual Support:** Generate content in **English**, **Arabic**, or **Bilingual (En/Ar)** formats.

## 5. Categorization & Visibility
Ensuring the right people see the right content is critical for security and relevance.

### Categorization
- **Target Department:** Assign the article to a specific department (e.g., Housekeeping, F&B).
- **Category:** Sub-categorize within the department for better organization.

### Visibility Settings
- **All Properties:** Visible to every employee in the company.
- **This Property Only:** Restricted to employees at your specific hotel location.
- **This Department Only:** Only members of the assigned department can view the article.
- **Specific Role:** Restricted to certain job levels (e.g., only "Managers").

## 6. Submission & Review Workflow
PRIME Hotels maintains high quality through a mandatory review process for specific roles.

1.  **Draft:** Save your work without others seeing it.
2.  **Submit for Review:** 
    - If you are a **Department Head** or **Property HR**, your article will be sent to the **Review Queue**.
    - Reviewers (General Managers or Regional Admins) will be notified immediately.
3.  **Status Tracking:**
    - `Draft`: Only you can see it.
    - `Pending Review`: Waiting for manager approval.
    - `Published`: Live and visible to the target audience.
    - `Rejected`: Needs corrections based on feedback.

---

## 7. The Review Queue (For Managers)
Managers responsible for quality control can access the **Content Review Queue**.

### Review Actions:
- **Approve & Publish:** The article goes live immediately.
- **Request Changes:** Send the article back to the author with specific comments on what to improve.
- **Reject:** Archive the article if it is no longer relevant or incorrect.

**Feedback Loop:** Always provide clear comments when requesting changes or rejecting an article to help the author understand the required standards.

---

## 8. Acknowledgment & Compliance
For "High-Stakes" documents (like Safety Protocols or Legal Policies), you can enable **"Requires Acknowledgment"**.

- **Staff View:** When reading the article, staff will see a prominent "I Acknowledge" button.
- **Tracking:** The system logs the exact timestamp when each employee acknowledges they have read and understood the document.
- **Analytics:** Managers can view compliance reports to see which team members have yet to complete their required reading.
