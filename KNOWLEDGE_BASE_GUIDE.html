1. System Architecture & Strategy

Every article in PRIME Connect is treated as an "Asset." This means articles are searchable, trackable, and version-controlled. When you create an article, you are contributing to our institutional memory.

Logic: The system automatically indexes your content words. If you use keywords in your title like "Fire Alarm" or "VIP Check-in," they will appear at the top of search results globally.

The AI is your personal content strategist. It is trained on hotel industry standards.

6. The Review Queue Lifecycle

All articles go through Peer Review to ensure accuracy.

  • Draft: Draft status. Only you see it. No notifications are sent.
  • Submission: Status changes to Pending Review. An email notification goes to the GM.
  • Decision: Managers use the "Review Queue" dashboard to check for technical accuracy. Feedback entered by the manager is sent back to the author via a system notification.
  • Published: Status becomes Published. The article is now searchable by the target audience.

7. Mandatory Acknowledgment & Auditing

For safety protocols, the Acknowledge feature is mandatory.

Auditor's Note: When an employee clicks "I Acknowledge," the system generates a non-editable record containing the User Name, Department, Date, Time, and IP Address. This record is used as proof of training during official property audits.
